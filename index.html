Data deletion request

How CatchApp users can request deletion of their account and personal data, as required for Google Play Data Safety disclosures.

About this page

CatchApp is committed to protecting user privacy and providing a clear process for requesting account and data deletion.

App name CatchApp
Request method Email request
Processing time Within 24 Hours (Mon-Fri)

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email requesting account deletion for your CatchApp account.
  2. Include the email address or account details linked to your CatchApp profile so we can identify your account correctly.
  3. Our support team will review the request and permanently delete the relevant account data within 24 Hours (Mon-Fri).
Send your request to: catchapp@afri-com.net
Important: Once your request has been processed, your CatchApp account and associated personal data will be permanently deleted, subject to any legal or security retention obligations that may apply.

This URL is referenced from the CatchApp Google Play listing for account and data deletion. Deletion is handled as described on this page and in our Privacy Policy.
